Create design philosophies - aesthetic movements expressed VISUALLY. Output .md, .pdf, and .png files.

Steps:
1. Design Philosophy Creation (.md file)
2. Express on canvas (.pdf or .png)

## DESIGN PHILOSOPHY CREATION

Create a VISUAL PHILOSOPHY interpreted through form, space, color, composition. Philosophy should be 4-6 paragraphs emphasizing: Visual expression. Spatial communication. Artistic interpretation. Minimal words.

Name the movement (1-2 words): "Brutalist Joy" / "Chromatic Silence" / "Metabolist Dreams"

Guidelines:
- Avoid redundancy
- Emphasize craftsmanship repeatedly (meticulously crafted, product of deep expertise, painstaking attention, master-level execution)
- Leave creative space

## CANVAS CREATION

With philosophy established, express on canvas. Create museum/magazine quality work:
- One single page, highly visual, design-forward PDF or PNG
- Use repeating patterns and perfect shapes
- Sparse, clinical typography
- Limited, intentional color palette
- Nothing falls off page, nothing overlaps

Text is always minimal. Use different fonts - search `./canvas-fonts` directory.

**CRITICAL**: Work must look like it took countless hours by someone at the absolute top of their field. Double-check: no overlaps, flawless formatting, every detail perfect.

## FINAL STEP

After creating: refine without adding more graphics. Make existing composition more cohesive. Second pass to make it a philosophically designed masterpiece.

## MULTI-PAGE

When requested: create additional pages as unique twists of original. Bundle in same .pdf or multiple .pngs.
